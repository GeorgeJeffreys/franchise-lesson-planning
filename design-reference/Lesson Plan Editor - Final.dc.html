Instructions for the teacher

Fist to Five
Students show how well they understood, 0–5.
✓ Added
How: Pause, ask the question, count to three, then everyone shows at once so no one copies. Note any pair below 3.

Whiteboard / Show Me

✓ Added
How: Pose one clear prompt. Give 20 seconds. Say “show me” so all reveal at once. Note who is unsure.
